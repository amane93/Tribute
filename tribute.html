Nikola Teslas Timeline

  • 1859: July 10th Nikola Tesla is born.
  • 1870: Tnikola contracted cholera and bearly escaped a life and death situation.
  • 1875: Enrolls in College.
  • 1880: Tesla moves to Prague to study philosophy
  • 1881: Tesla begins his career as Chief Electrical Engineer.
  • 1882: Tesla goes to work for Edison.
  • 1887: Tesla re-imagines the electric motor.
  • 1890: Nikola begins his work on wireless transmission.
  • 1891: Tesla patents the tesla coil.
  • 1943: Tesla's untimely demise. A loss for humanity and progress.
